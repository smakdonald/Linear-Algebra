Returning to our example of fitting a straight line to our set \(\set{(x_i,y_i)}_1^N\) of points we see that \(\vv{p}=\proj_{\range(M)}\vv{y}\) is the closest point in \(\range(M)\) to \(\vv{y}\text{.}\) Thus to solve for \(\alpha\) and \(B\) we should solve \(M\begin{bmatrix} \alpha \\\beta \end{bmatrix} = \vv{p}\text{.}\)
