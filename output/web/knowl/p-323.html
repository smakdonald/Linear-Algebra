We can even have infinite direct sums: suppose that \((V_i)_{i\in I}\) is a family of subspaces of a vector space \(W\) and any vector \(\w=\v_1+\cdots+\v_n\) with \(\v_i\in V_i\) can be written uniquely in this form we say we have a direct sum \(\bigoplus_{i\in I} V_i\text{.}\)
