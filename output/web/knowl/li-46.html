Item 3.

\(P:\R^2\to \R^2\) where \(P\) is a projection as in [cross-reference to target(s) "ex:projection" missing or not unique] is NOT surjective. However if we change the target of the map to be the line \(L\) onto which we project then \(P:\R^2\to L\) is surjective. The moral here is that whether a map is surjective or not depends on its codomain.
