A basis for \(V_{n-1}\text{,}\) the subspace of polynomials of degree at most \(n-1\) in \(\F[X]\text{,}\) is \(B=\{1, X,\ldots, X^{n-1}\}\text{.}\) We will use the standard basis \(B'\) for \(\F^n\text{.}\) Then the matrix for the evaluation map in these bases is the Vandermonde matrix
\begin{equation*} [\text{ev}]_B^{B'}=\begin{pmatrix} 1 & x_1 & \cdots & x_1^{n-1} \\ 1 & x_2 & \cdots & x_2^{n-1} \\ \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots\\ 1 & x_n & \cdots & x_{n}^{n-1} \end{pmatrix}. \end{equation*}
