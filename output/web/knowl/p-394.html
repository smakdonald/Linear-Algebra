Suppose a lienarly set \(I\) of \(V\) has \(d\) elements. By [cross-reference to target(s) "thm:dim" missing or not unique] (3), \(I\) is contained a basis \(B\) and by [cross-reference to target(s) "thm:dim" missing or not unique] (1) \(B\) has \(d=\dim V\) elements. Since \(B\) has \(d\) elements the only subset of \(B\) that has \(d\) elements is \(B\) itself so \(I=B\text{.}\) Thus \(I\) is a basis since \(B\) was.
