If \(W\) is a subspace of \(V\) the it is possible to make the collection of all affine subspaces parallel to \(W\) into a vector space called the quotient vector space \(V/W\text{.}\) We let:
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} V / W &\defeq \setof{A}{\text{$A$ is an affine subspace of $V$ parallel to $W$}} \\ A + B &\defeq \setof{\vv{a}+\b}{\vv{a}\in A, \b\in B}, && \text{for $A,B \in V/W$} \\ \l A &\defeq \begin{cases} \setof{ \l \vv{a}}{\vv{a}\in A} & \text{$\l \not= 0$} \\ W & \l=0, \end{cases} && \text{for $A\in V/W$, $\l\in\F$.} \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
