One important thing to notice is that the step \((2,-1)\) might occur when moving from the point \((1,1)\) to the point \((3,0)\text{,}\) or when moving from the point \((7,10)\) to the point \((9,9)\text{,}\) or many other possibilities.  1  In other words, steps don't have a fixed position, but do have a length and a direction.
