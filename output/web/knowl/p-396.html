Let \(B_W\) be a basis of \(W\text{.}\) Then \(B_W\) is a linearly independent subset of \(W\) and hence also of \(V\) since \(W\of V\text{.}\) By [cross-reference to target(s) "thm:dim" missing or not unique] (3) \(V\) has a basis \(B_V\) so that \(B_W\of B_V\text{.}\) Since \(B_V\) is finite, so is \(B_W\) and moreover \(\dim W=\# B_W\leq B_V=\dim V\text{.}\)
