If \(\u = (x, y)\) and \(\w=(x', y')\) are vectors in \(\R^2\) then we define their dot product to be
\begin{equation*} \u \cdot \w = x x' + y y' = \abs{\u} \, \abs{\w} \cos(\theta), \end{equation*}
where \(\theta\) is the angle between \(\u\) and \(\v\) and \(\abs{\u} , \abs{\w}\) denote the length of \(\u\) and of \(\w\text{.}\)
