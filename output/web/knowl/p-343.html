The fact that \(\range(S)\) is all of \(U+W\) is clear. To prove that \(\ker(S)=\set{(\0,\0)}\) consider a pair \((\u,\w)\in U \dsum W\) such that \(\u+\w=\0\text{.}\) We need to show that \(\u=\w=\0\text{.}\) Well, since \(\u=-\w\) we have \(\u\in U\cap W=\set{\0}\) so \(\u=0\) and hence \(\w=\0\text{.}\)
