\((1)\Rightarrow (2)\) Suppose \(T\) is injective. Then \(\ker(T)=\{0_V\}\) has \(\dim \ker(T)=0\text{.}\) By Rank-Nullity, it follows that \(\dim \range(T)=\dim V=\dim W\text{.}\) Since \(\range(T)\subseteq W\) and \(\dim \range(T)=\dim W\) we conclude \(\range(T)=W\text{,}\) in other words \(T\) is surjective.
