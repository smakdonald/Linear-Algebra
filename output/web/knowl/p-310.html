The Lagrange basis for the vector space \(V_{n-1}\) with respect to the nodes \(x_1,\ldots,x_n\in \F\) consists of the polynomials
\begin{equation*} \ell_i(x)=\prod_{1\leq j\leq n, j\neq i} \frac{x-x_j}{x_i-x_j} \end{equation*}
which satisfy
\begin{equation*} \label{eq:lagrange} \ell_i(x_j)=\begin{cases} 0 & i\neq j\\ 1 & i=j. \end{cases} \end{equation*}
