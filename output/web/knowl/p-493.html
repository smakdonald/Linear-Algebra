First note that there is, by [cross-reference to target(s) "lem:UUperp" missing or not unique], a unique way of writing \(\vv{y}= \vv{p}+\w\) with \(\vv{p}\in U\text{,}\) \(\w\in U\perp\text{.}\) Now suppose that \(\u\) is any element of \(U\text{.}\) We have
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} \norm{\vv{y}-\u}^2 &= \norm{(\vv{y}-\vv{p})+(\vv{p}-\u)}^2 \\ &= \norm{\vv{y}-\vv{p}}^2 + \norm{\vv{p}-\u}^2+ 2\ip{\vv{y}-\vv{p},\vv{p}-\u}\\ &\ge \norm{\vv{y}-\vv{p}}^2, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
with equality only when \(\u=\vv{p}\text{.}\) The second equality in the above display uses the fact that \(\vv{y}-\vv{p}\in U\perp\) and \(\vv{p}-\u\in U\text{.}\)
