How does a mathematician play the game? The states of the board are in one-to-one correspondence with the vector space \(V = M_{5\by 5}(\F_2)\text{.}\) Define \(M_{i j}\) to be the element of \(V\) with \(1\)s in every entry of row \(i\) and also in every entry of column \(j\) and with all other entries \(0\text{.}\)
