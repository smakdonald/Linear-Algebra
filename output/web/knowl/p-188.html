Linear independence has to do with whether one can write the same vector \(\w\in \linspan(S)\) as a linear combination of vectors in \(S\) in two different ways
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} \sum_{i=1}^na_i\v_i =\w =\sum_{i=1}^nb_i\v_i. \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
This leads to considering an equation of the form \(\sum_{i=1}^n(a_i-b_i)\v_i=\0\text{.}\)
