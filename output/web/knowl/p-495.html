If \(\ip{,}\) is an inner product on a finite dimensional vector space \(V\) and \(f\in V\dual\) then there exists a unique vector \(\v_f\in V\) such that for all \(\w\in V\) we have
\begin{equation*} f(\w) = \ip{\w,\v_f}. \end{equation*}
Moreover, the map \(\varphi:V^*\to V\) given by \(\varphi(f)\mapsto \v_f\) is a bijection.
