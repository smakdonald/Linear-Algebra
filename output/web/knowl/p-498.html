Now if \(f\in V^*\text{,}\) then \((\psi\circ\varphi)(f)=\psi(\sum_1^n\overline{f(\v_i)} \v_i)\) is the unique linear that maps \(\v_i \mapsto \overline{\overline{f(\v_i)}}=f(\v_i)\text{.}\) But \(f\) also maps \(v_i\mapsto f(\v_i)\) so by uniqueness we have \((\psi\circ\varphi)(f)=f\text{.}\)
