Let \(W=M_{n\times n} (\R)\) and let \(U\) and \(V\) be the subspaces of symmetric and antisymmetric matrices respectively. In detail \(A\in U\) if and only if \(A=A^T\) and \(A\in V\) if and only if \(A=-A^T\text{,}\) where \(T\) denotes transpose. Then \(W=U\oplus V\) as we can write
\begin{equation*} A=\frac{1}{2}(A+A^T) +\frac{1}{2}(A-A^T) \end{equation*}
and moreover if \(A=B+C\) with \(B\in U\) and \(C\in V\) then we have \(A^T=B^T+C^T=B-C\text{.}\) From
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} A &=B+C\\ A^T&=B-C \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
we obtain \(B=\frac{1}{2}(A+A^T)\) and \(C=\frac{1}{2}(A-A^T)\text{.}\)
