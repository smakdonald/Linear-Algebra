The least squares problem is then
\begin{equation*} \label{leastsquares} \begin{cases} \text{ minimize } \norm{\vv{y}-\vv{p}}^2=(y_1-p_1)^2+\cdots +(y_n-p_n)^2\\ \text{ subject to } \vv{p}\in \left\{ M\begin{bmatrix} \alpha \\ \beta \end{bmatrix} \mid \alpha, \beta \in \R \right\}=:U. \end{cases} \end{equation*}
