There is an electronic game called Lights Out released by Tiger Toys in 1995. The game board consists of twenty-five lights in a \(5\by 5\) grid. Pushing any of the lights causes that light to switch state (from on to off or vice versa) and also causes all the lights in the same row or column as the one being pushed to switch state. One is presented with a pattern of lights, and the objective is to press some of the lights so as to switch all the lights off.
