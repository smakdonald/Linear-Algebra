Suppose a spanning set \(S\) of \(V\) has \(d\) elements. By [cross-reference to target(s) "thm:dim" missing or not unique] (2), \(S\) contains a basis \(B\) and by [cross-reference to target(s) "thm:dim" missing or not unique] (1) \(B\) has \(d=\dim V\) elements. Since \(S\) has \(d\) elements the only subset of \(S\) that has \(d\) elements is \(S\) itself so \(S=B\text{.}\) Thus \(S\) is a basis since \(B\) was.
