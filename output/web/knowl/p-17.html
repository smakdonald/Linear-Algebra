Now we have a second question. How do we specify the direction of the axis \(L\text{?}\) Having spent so much time thinking about vectors, let's do it according to a vector. Let's pick a vector \(\w\) that's in the direction of this axis. Many vectors specify this same direction: if \(\w\) works then so does \(170\w\text{,}\) or \(0.1\w\text{.}\)
