More generally, if \(V_1,\ldots, V_n\) are subspaces of a vector space \(W\) we say that \(V_1+\cdots+ V_n\) is an internal direct sum and we denote it \(V_1\oplus \cdots \oplus V_n\) if every vector \(\w\in V_1+\cdots+ V_n\) can be written uniquely as \(\w=\v_1+\cdots+\v_n\) with \(\v_i\in V_i\text{.}\)
