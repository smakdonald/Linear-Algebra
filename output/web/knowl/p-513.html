If \(\v,\w\in V\text{,}\) an inner product space, then we say that two subspaces \(U,W\) of \(V\) are orthogonal if \(\u,\w\) are orthogonal for all \(\u\in U\text{,}\) \(\w\in W\text{.}\) If \(V=U\dsum W\) and \(U,W\) are orthogonal then we write
\begin{equation*} V = U \odsum W. \qedhere \end{equation*}
