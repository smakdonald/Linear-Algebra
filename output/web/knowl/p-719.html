If \(A\in M_{n\by n}(\R)\) is a symmetric matrix then \(T=M_A:\R^n\to \R^n\) is self-adjoint, where we condider \(\R^n\) with its standard inner product. We have
\begin{equation*} \ip{T\v,\w} = \ip{A\v,\w} = (A\v)\trans \w = \v A\trans \w = \ip{\v,A\trans \w} = \ip{\v,A\w}. \end{equation*}
