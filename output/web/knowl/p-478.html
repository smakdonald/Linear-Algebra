Suppose \(U\cap V=\{\0_W\}\text{.}\) If \(\u+\v=\u'+\v'\) with \(\u,\u'\in U\) and \(\v, \v'\in V\) then \(\u-\u'=\v'-\v\text{.}\) The left hand side is in \(U\) and the right hand side is in \(V\text{.}\) So we must have \(\u-\u'=\v'-\v\in U\cap V\text{,}\) therefore \(\u-\u'=\v'-\v=\0\text{.}\) So \(\u=\u'\) and \(\v=\v'\text{.}\)
